Dog Life

The feeding time must be accurate, try to feed them according to the habits of the original owner, and do not feed them too much sweet, salty and irritating food. The newborn baby needs to be fed by the owner himself. Over time, a deep bond can be established with the pet dog, and mutual trust can be deepened. Another important task of the owner is to help the little one overcome the pain of leaving his mother and adapt to the new environment as soon as possible.

Cat Life

We can't keep the kitten at home all the time, we need to take the kitten out for a walk from time to time, and then, when we raise the kitten, we need to play with the kitten more. When we raise a kitten, we also need to take care of its hair more.
